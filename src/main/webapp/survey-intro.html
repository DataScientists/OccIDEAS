WELCOME TO THE WELDERS NOISE APP
Welcome to our app which helps welders find out how much noise they are exposed to at work. Our app will ask you questions about the sorts of tasks you usually do at work. From that, our app estimates the noise you will be exposed to at work. Please take your time to carefully read the questions before you answer them.
COMPLETING THE SURVEY
The questionnaire should take about 10 minutes to complete. As you move through the questions, please use the Save and Continue buttons - do not use your browser buttons.
More information about who is doing this survey and the background can be found here
If you have any questions, please email {{results.emailLink}}
CONFIDENTIALITY
It is important to note that your participation in this survey is completely voluntary and at any time you can choose to exit this questionnaire. No data that identifies you as an individual are being collected.
The answers you provide are completely confidential and anonymous. Your personal noise result will be reported back to you. We will also provide you with a comparison with similar workers so you can see how the noise level at your work compares with other similar workers. In order to do this, we would like your permission to store the results of your questionnaire anonymously.
